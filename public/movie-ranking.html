My Top 3 Movies of all times

Mission Mangal

This movie is about India's quest to launch a module to space which goes to Mars and give us valuable information about it. Despite ISRO's failure earlier , it reiterated and turmoiled very hard to achieve this feat and India became one of the nations who could send its satellite to Mars in record budget which was less than the hollywood's movie 'Gravity'

Super 30

The story hails from an ambition of an Indian boy who wanted to go Harvad University and he did receive an offer letter too , but due to odd times and his father's demise , he didn't go . But this led him to the foundation of Super 30 - a batch of 30 students , whom he taught the curriculum of the entrance exams IIT JEE and all these students were poor but were ambitious enough . Their passion led them in clearing the most difficult entrance exams of India and they entered into IITs

Oppenheimer

This is a thriller film produced by Christopher Nolan which talks about the life of Robert . J. Oppenheimer , who was a scientist who changed the world with his theory on atom bombs. The film fantasizes about his quest for producing a chain reaction that would probably not stop ever and would destroy the world ! This film goes deeper into his political and social life and his contradictions with Lewis Strauss , who was then found guilty and was arrested.
